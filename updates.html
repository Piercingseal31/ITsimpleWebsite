We Are Accepting OJTs!

Good day! At IT SIMPLE, we believe learning goes beyond the classroom. That's why we're thrilled to welcome our new On-the-Job Training (OJT) students.

Through this program, trainees gain hands on experience in IT operations—from troubleshooting and system management to workflow optimization and project collaboration. It's a chance to apply classroom knowledge to real-world situations.

Our goal is to prepare OJT students for future careers in technology by providing practical experience, guidance, and exposure to industry practices.

Ready to grow in your IT journey? At IT SIMPLE, we're here to support your success.
